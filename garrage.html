INCREDIBLE GARAGES WITH SWIZZ BEATZ

In a new six-part series, we meet the Ferrari collectors whose keen eye for art has created some inspirational - and wild - car ports

Designed by Wallace Cunningham, the iconic Razor House is home to record producer, DJ, rapper
brand songwriter Swizz Beatz. The music aficionado finds the setting of the house, along the
Californian coast, serves as the perfect backdrop to his Ferrari collection, which includes a
side-by-side Monza SP1 and SP2. “My goal is to have all the SPs,” Beatz says, adding that he
has seen many collections, but few collectors “really collect properly”.
